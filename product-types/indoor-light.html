60 Watt Down Light (2X2 Clean Room)

The 60 Watt Down Light (2X2 Clean Room) is designed for environments requiring precision lighting, such as pharmaceuticals and hospitals. With a high lumen output (≥ 6000 lm) and low power consumption, it ensures energy efficiency and consistent performance. Built with CRCA powder-coated housing and advanced LED technology, it offers durability, reliability, and an elegant design.

Model No: NT-2X2DL-60S

Electrical Parameters:

  • Power : 60Watt

  • Input Voltage :90-270VAC
  • Power Factor : ≥0.95
  • THD : ≤10%
  • Lumens Output : ≥ 6000 lm
  • Surge : 2.5 KV
  • Protection : No load, Short Circuit

Led Parameters

  • CRI : ≥ 80
  • LED Type Mid Power
  • LED Make Everlight/Bridgelux/ Samsung
  • LED Efficacy: 125 lm/watt
  • CCT 3000K/4000K/ 5700K/6500K
  • CRI : ≥ 80

Mechnical Parameters

  • Shape : Square

  • Outer Diameter : 610X610MM
  • Cutout Diameter : 570X570MM
